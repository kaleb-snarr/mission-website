About Me & The Church

My Name is Kaleb Snarr. And I am from South Jordan Utah. And I am a Missionary for my church. When you think about Utah, one of the first things you may think about is "Mormons" and you might be wondering, what is a Mormon? Well that is exactly what I am here for. A "Mormon" is someone who is a member of the "Mormon" Religion. A couple things Members Of the Church believe include:

  • Jesus Christ, God, & The Holy Ghost
  • Baptism
  • Church Attendance
  • Prayer
  • Priesthood
  • Scripture

The Term "Mormon" is often used to describe the members of the church of Jesus Christ of latter day Saints (which is the correct term.) The members of which believe in Jesus Christ as our savior and redeemer. We believe in God (our heavenly father) and the holy ghost. as well as the bible and book of Mormon being scripture and doctrine. We also follow prophets, one of which was Joseph Smith who found and translated the Book Of Mormon.

Our Purpose As Missionaries

Our Search

Our goal is to seek out people who are willing to learn about the gosple that we care so much about. It has brought me happiness thus I want to offer the opportunity for others to feel the same.

Travel & Time

As Missionaries, we have the opportunity to leave our homes when we turn 18 (19 for women) for 18 months-2 years to share the gospel we believe in. We get called to a place which could be almost anywhere in the world. I for example got called to the Santo Domingo east mission Dominican Republic.

Spread Love

We are taught as Missionaries to have love for everyone around us, we are expected to be kind and treat others and thier cultures with respect as we pursue our goal of teaching the gospel of Jesus Christ. This is one of the most important parts of being a Missionary.

Study

Study is a major part of being a Missionary. We even receive training and attend classes prior to leaving for our mission locations. We are expected to be very knowledgable about the gospel so we can teach effectively. Sometimes we are expected to learn a new language for the purpose of teaching as well.

Service

Another big part of serving a mission is service. service meaning helping out the communities in which we stay for our missions. We find joy in helping other people because it's how we can show what we strive to be, which is like Jesus.

Listen

Our job is to teach but its also to learn. As a Missionary, we are very open to cultures that we are unfamiliar with, people we are unfamiliar with, and places we are unfamiliar with. We want to teach but we want to learn just as much!
